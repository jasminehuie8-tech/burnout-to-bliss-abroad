Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to me. This Privacy Policy explains how Burnout to Bliss Abroad ("I", "me", "my") — operated by Coleen Huie Garcia — collects, uses, and protects information when you visit burnouttoblissdigital.com or purchase any of my products or services.

1. Information I Collect

I may collect the following information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, and any details you voluntarily provide through contact forms or call bookings.
  • Purchase information — transaction details processed securely through Stripe or Gumroad. I do not store your credit card information.
  • Usage data — general analytics such as pages visited and time spent on the site, collected through standard website analytics tools.
  • Email communications — if you subscribe to my newsletter or email list, I collect your email address and any information you provide.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and fulfill your purchases
  • Send you emails, updates, and relevant content you have opted into
  • Improve the website and my services
  • Schedule and manage discovery calls

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

This site uses third-party platforms to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Gumroad — digital product delivery (gumroad.com/privacy)
  • Calendly — call scheduling (calendly.com/privacy)
  • Skool — community platform (skool.com/privacy)

4. Cookies

This website may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

5. Email Communications

If you subscribe to my email list, you may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email. I will not send you unsolicited communications.

6. Data Security

I take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. By using this site, you acknowledge and accept this risk.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

I reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, email me at sales@burnouttoblissdigital.com.
